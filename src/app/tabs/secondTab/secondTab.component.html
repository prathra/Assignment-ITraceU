⚙️ Cricket Technology & Stats

Technology has transformed cricket from a traditional sport to a modern, data-driven spectacle. From real-time decision tools to advanced predictive analytics, the game is smarter and sharper than ever before.

📍 Hawk-Eye

Visual tracking system that predicts ball trajectory and assists umpires in LBW and boundary decisions.

🔊 Snickometer

Audio-based technology that detects faint edges using microphone spikes synced with ball movements.

🧠 Data Analytics

Helps teams analyze player performance, win probabilities, pitch behavior, and match simulations.
