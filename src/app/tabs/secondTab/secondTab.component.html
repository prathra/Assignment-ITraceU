Cricket Technology & Stats

Modern cricket has embraced technology like never before. Tools like Hawk-Eye, Snickometer, and DRS (Decision Review System) have revolutionized decision-making on the field.

Stats now play a critical role in strategy. Data analysis helps teams understand player form, pitch conditions, and even bowling patterns.

  • Hawk-Eye: Tracks ball trajectory for LBW decisions
  • Snickometer: Detects faint edges using audio spikes
  • DRS: Empowers players to challenge umpire calls
  • Analytics: Player stats, win probabilities, heat maps
