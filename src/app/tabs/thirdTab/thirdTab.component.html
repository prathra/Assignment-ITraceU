Cricket Legends

Cricket has been blessed with players who redefined the sport with their skill, passion, and dedication. Here are some of the most legendary cricketers of all time:

  • 🏏 Sachin Tendulkar (India): The 'Master Blaster', with 100 international centuries
  • 🏏 Sir Donald Bradman (Australia): Holds the highest batting average in Test history (99.94)
  • 🏏 Muttiah Muralitharan (Sri Lanka): Most wickets in Test and ODI cricket

These legends continue to inspire generations of cricket lovers around the globe.
